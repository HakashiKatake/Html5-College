You can make part of a paragraph a clickable link by wrapping the desired text in an <a> tag. For example: click here to visit the website.

The <strong> tag is used to indicate that the text is of strong importance, and it is semantically meaningful. The <b> tag is used to make text bold without adding semantic importance. For example: Important text vs. Bold text.
